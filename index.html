Washington D.C.

October 16, 2019

Washington D.C. is the capital city of the United States, which was named after George Washington, the first President of the United States. Washington D.C. has a lot to see in terms of architecture and memorials. I visited this beautiful city last year. I have been to the White House, Washington Monument, Lincoln Memorial, National Gallery of Art, and the U.S. Capitol. I would never grow bored of visiting this city and would definitely go back to visit one day.

Las Vegas

November 21, 2019

Ahhh, Las Vegas!!! I love this city. This city in the desert is known around the world for fun and excitement. You'll find an always impressive list of shows, and beautiful and luxurious hotels. Las Vegas is a great place to visit year-round. The most famouse attraction is The Strip, which is lined with world-class entertainment palaces, and home to performance venue and fine dining. To truly experience Las Vegas, stay on The Strip and enjoy the Aria buffet.

Vancouver

May 15, 2018

I was so excited to visit Vancouver with my friend. Vancouver is perfectly positioned between the ocean and the mountains. There is plenty to do within the city. You can go shopping in the downtown core and bike around Stanley Park. The park offers spectacular views of the North Shore mountains and Lions Gate Bridge.
